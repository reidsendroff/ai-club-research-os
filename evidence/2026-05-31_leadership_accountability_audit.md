## Finding

A prior review concluded that the "run the club like a company" instinct is directionally right, but the phrase and raw corporate surface language are risky for a high school club. The stronger framing is a "Leadership Operating System" with role charters, artifact-based scorecards, routine feedback, a student-safe Leadership Improvement Plan, advisor-visible role transitions, and privacy boundaries.

## Key Audit Points

- The June 1 meeting plan became too long after adding a 10-minute accountability section.
- "PIP" language should not be member-facing.
- Secretary should not hold confidential officer performance notes.
- Follower growth is a weak CMO metric compared with on-time, specific, high-quality posts.
- Presidents also need accountability standards.
- The full framework belongs in Strategic Plan / AI_Club_OS, while June 1 should receive only a short version.

## Limits

The audit was based on local files. Live Google Drive context was not reviewed.
