# 05 Constraints

## Research OS Anti-scope

Do not build RAG, vector DB, embeddings retrieval, LangChain, LlamaIndex, CrewAI, AutoGen, Semantic Kernel, RL, fine-tuning, learned routing, a custom server, webhook dispatch, autonomous agent orchestration, or anything requiring API keys to set up. Coordination is Markdown plus human judgment.

## AI Club Constraints

- The human orchestrator controls which agent runs next.
- Agents write scratchpads. Humans curate canonical memory.
- No school-facing communication goes out without Reid and Ben review.
- Any administration-facing or Mr. Novak-facing claim must be direct, specific, and evidence-backed.
- The AI Club tone rules apply: no corporate padding, no generic hype, no fake polish, no motivational closers.
- Student accountability policies must be fair, private, advisor-visible when role removal is possible, and not publicly humiliating.
- The missing AI Club Leadership Google Drive link is a material evidence gap.
- Member data, attendance, discipline, and finance records may be sensitive. Do not expose or invent them.
- Claims about current competitions, deadlines, model releases, or school rules require current verification before publication.

## Evidence Rules

Every non-trivial factual claim must be tagged:

- `[src: evidence/YYYY-MM-DD_slug.md]`
- `[PRIOR]`
- `[INFERENCE]`
- `[UNVERIFIED]`

When unsure, write `[UNVERIFIED]`. Never fabricate citations.

## Feasibility Gate

Before proposing new software, dashboards, or automated systems, first prove the club has enough reliable source data and owners to maintain them.
