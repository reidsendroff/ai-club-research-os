# 06 Glossary

artifact_id: Stable id for a club artifact, policy, meeting, role, team, evidence file, or decision.

AI Club OS: The 12-file operating system under `AI_Club_OS/`.

canonical memory: Human-curated project memory in `memory/canonical/`.

CMO: Social media / Chief Marketing Officer role. Owns announcement and content system.

evidence file: A durable source-backed finding in `evidence/`.

Leadership OS: Proposed name for the role-charter, scorecard, review, improvement, and succession system.

Leadership Improvement Plan: Student-safe replacement for corporate "PIP" language.

Room 133: AI Club meeting and proposed open-lab room.

scratchpad: Agent-written latest output in `memory/scratchpads/`.

scrum: Bi-weekly team update format where project teams show progress, blockers, and next commitments.

three-post system: AI Club announcement cadence: 7AM hook, 1PM reminder, and You Missed It recap.

VP/COO: Operations officer role. Owns meeting logistics, scrums, team liaison work, and blockers.
