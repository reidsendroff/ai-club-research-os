## Finding

The plan is to shift the AI Club from "Reid runs the club" to "systems run the club." The core mechanisms are:

- 5 project teams of about 4 members each.
- Bi-weekly scrums where every team presents shipped work, blocker, solution or current stuck point, and next commitment.
- Room 133 open lab three days per week.
- 8 officer positions: 2 VP/COO, 2 CMO, 2 Treasurer, 2 Secretary.
- 75 percent participation minimum for members.
- Leadership roles with distinct ownership and accountability standards.
- Parent donation drive to fund project teams.
- Competition targets including Congressional App Challenge, MIT Blueprint, FBLA, and others.

## Success Benchmark

The plan defines success as at least 3 project teams submitting to national competitions, consistent Room 133 open lab attendance, a working announcement system, clean officer succession, at least one member-built tool reaching users outside school, and Reid/Ben being able to miss significant meetings without the club degrading.

## Limits

The plan is documented but not yet empirically validated with live 2026-2027 operating data.
